Why Learn CYBER SECURITY ?

Protecting Sensitive Data:Cybersecurity measures are implemented to protect sensitive data, including personal information, financial records, intellectual property, and classified government information, from unauthorized access, theft, or disclosure.

Securing Networks and Systems:Cybersecurity technologies and practices are employed to secure computer networks, systems, and devices from various cyber threats, such as malware, ransomware, viruses, and phishing attacks.

Ensuring Privacy and Compliance:Cybersecurity helps organizations comply with data protection regulations and standards, such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

Preventing Cyber Attacks and Breaches:Cybersecurity measures are implemented to prevent and mitigate cyber attacks and data breaches, which can have significant financial, reputational, and operational consequences for organizations

Detecting and Responding to Security Incidents:Cybersecurity involves continuous monitoring of network traffic, system logs, and security events to detect and respond to security incidents in real-time.
